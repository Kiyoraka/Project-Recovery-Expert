About Us

Company Background

Recovery Xpert Sdn Bhd is a newly established yet professionally led Malaysian-based debt collection agency. Formed in 2025 by seasoned entrepreneurs Mr. Nivas Raguavan and Mr. Kumaresh, the company's focus is on redefining debt recovery with integrity, professionalism, and innovation. Our goal is to empower clients—ranging from SMEs to corporates, co-operatives, and government-linked companies—to recover outstanding debts with maximum efficiency while preserving business relationships.

Vision

To be Malaysia's most trusted, ethical, and results-driven debt recovery agency, combining human insight and technology for sustainable financial outcomes.

Mission

  • Deliver end-to-end receivables management with transparency and empathy.
  • Preserve client reputation while transforming dues swiftly and lawfully.
  • Create value through digital transformation and strategic recovery processes.
