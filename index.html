About Us

Company Background

Recovery Xpert Sdn Bhd is a newly established yet professionally led Malaysian-based debt collection agency. Formed in 2025 by seasoned entrepreneurs Mr. Nivas Raguavan and Mr. Kumaresh, the company's focus is on redefining debt recovery with integrity, professionalism, and innovation. Our goal is to empower clients—ranging from SMEs to corporates, co-operatives, and government-linked companies—to recover outstanding debts with maximum efficiency while preserving business relationships.

Vision

To be Malaysia's most trusted, ethical, and results-driven debt recovery agency, combining human insight and technology for sustainable financial outcomes.

Mission

  • Deliver end-to-end receivables management with transparency and empathy.
  • Preserve client reputation while transforming dues swiftly and lawfully.
  • Create value through digital transformation and strategic recovery processes.

Lieutenant General Dato' Sri Azzuddin bin Ahmad

CHAIRMAN

Born 17 May 1946 in Alor Star, Kedah, Dato' Sri Azzuddin trained at the Malaysian Royal Military College and was commissioned 2nd Lieutenant in the Royal Malay Regiment on 17 Feb 1966. He served as Platoon Commander and Regimental Intelligence Officer before postings with the Joint Intelligence Directorate (later DISD) and the Prime Minister's Department.

Rising steadily through leadership roles—including State Military Intelligence Officer in Sarawak—he attended the Command and General Staff College, USA, and the Royal College of Defence Studies, UK. Promotions followed: Colonel (1991), Brigadier General and Chief Provost Marshal (1995), Major General and Director General Defence Intelligence (1999), and finally Lieutenant General.

A respected military intelligence officer, he forged strong ties with agencies and corporations in Malaysia and abroad, particularly across ASEAN, Asia, Turkey, Bosnia, and South Africa. A Master's degree holder in Defence Studies, he also excelled in sports, representing the Malaysian Armed Forces and the national football team.

Post-retirement, he served as General Secretary of the Football Association of Malaysia (2007–2013) and the ASEAN Football Federation (2013–2019). Honours for over three decades of national service include Dato' Setia DiRaja Kedah (DSDK), Johan Setia Mahkota (JSM), Kesatria Mangku Negara (KMN), Panglima Setia Angkatan Tentera (PSAT), Darjah Sultan Ahmad Shah Pahang (DSAP), and Sri Sultan Ahmad Shah Pahang (SSAP).

We are honoured to have LIEUTENANT GENERAL DATO' SRI AZZUDDIN BIN AHMAD (Retired) to lead this organisation as its Chairman.
